⏱️ Daily Start & Reach Guide

  • Best start on long days: 06:00–07:00 AM (D2, D6, D9, D11)
  • Day trips (D5, D10): Leave by 05:30–06:00 AM
  • Lunch stop: Add 45–60 min on drives over 5 hrs
  • Health breaks: Stop every 90 min for Mom, Dad & Hetal (15 min)
  • Mountain rule: Average 25–35 km/hr on hill roads

🚶 Trek Time (Car Stops Here)

  • Yamunotri (D3): 6 km one way · 3–4 hrs up · 2–3 hrs down
  • Kedarnath (D7): 16 km one way · 6–8 hrs up · 4–5 hrs down
  • Car stops at Janki Chatti (Yamunotri) / Sonprayag (Kedarnath) — Ertiga cannot go further
  • Pony/palkhi saves ~2–3 hrs each way for seniors

✈️ Day 1 & Day 12 Flight Buffer

  • D1: Land Delhi 09:15 AM → start road ~10:30 AM → Haridwar ~5 PM
  • D12: Haridwar 06:00 AM → Delhi ~12:00 PM → airport by 01:00 PM
  • Book return flight after 04:00 PM for safe buffer
  • IndiGo D1 confirmed · PNR GCCVTA

⚠️ May–June Delay Factors

  • Monsoon landslides on Rishikesh–Badrinath highway
  • Yatra traffic jams near temple towns (weekends worse)
  • Kedarnath / Yamunotri queue time not included
  • Road repair at Chopta, Joshimath belt — add 1–2 hrs buffer
✓ Ertiga for your family (4 + driver): Enough space for Mom, Dad, Hetal & luggage · AC for heat · stop anytime for sugar/BP checks · flexible timings vs tempo traveller. On steep days (D9, D11) allow an extra 15–30 min vs larger SUVs.
